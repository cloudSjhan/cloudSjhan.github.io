Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sync my data across devices?

Create an account (via email or Sign in with Apple) and your entries will automatically sync across all your devices signed into the same account.

Can I recover deleted entries?

Deleted entries are permanently removed and cannot be recovered. Please be careful when deleting entries.

How do I delete my account?

Go to the Profile tab, tap Settings, and select "Delete Account". This will permanently remove all your data from our servers.

Why does the app need photo library access?

Life Calendar needs access to your photo library so you can attach photos and videos to your journal entries. This permission is only used when you choose to add media.

How do I set up daily reminders?

Go to Profile > Settings and enable daily reminders. You can customize the reminder time to fit your schedule.
