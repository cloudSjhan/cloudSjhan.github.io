Privacy Policy for Life Calendar

Last Updated: May 6, 2026

Overview

Life Calendar is an iOS journaling application that helps you record daily moods, notes, and photos. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains what data Life Calendar collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

Data We Collect

Data You Provide

  • Journal Entries — Mood selections, text notes, and tags you create
  • Photos and Videos — Media you attach to your journal entries from your photo library
  • Account Information — Email address and display name when you create an account (via email or Sign in with Apple)
  • Profile Information — Optional avatar and cover image you upload

Data Collected Automatically

  • Device Identifier — A randomly generated UUID used solely for multi-device sync purposes
  • Timezone — Used to correctly display dates for your entries

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Store and display your journal entries
  • Sync your data across your devices
  • Provide "On This Day" memory features
  • Send optional daily reminder notifications (only if you enable them)

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, profiling, or any purpose other than providing the app's core functionality.

Data Storage and Sync

  • All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework
  • If you create an account, your data is synced to our backend service (hosted on Supabase) to enable multi-device access
  • Media files are stored in encrypted cloud storage accessible only to your account
  • Data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS encryption)

Third-Party Services

Supabase

We use Supabase as our backend service for user authentication, data storage and sync, and media file storage. Supabase processes data in accordance with their privacy policy. Your data is stored in secure, access-controlled databases.

Apple Sign In

If you choose to sign in with Apple, we receive only the information you authorize (email and name). Apple may provide a private relay email address.

Device Permissions

Life Calendar requests the following permissions:

  • Photo Library — To select photos and videos for your journal entries. Only accessed when you choose to add media.
  • Notifications — To send optional daily reminders. You can disable this at any time in Settings.

These permissions are managed by iOS and can be changed at any time in Settings > Life Calendar.

Data Retention

  • Your data is retained as long as you maintain an active account
  • You can delete individual entries at any time within the app
  • You can delete your entire account and all associated data from the app's settings

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (all data is visible within the app)
  • Delete individual entries or your entire account
  • Export your data (available in app settings)
  • Withdraw consent by deleting your account

Children's Privacy

Life Calendar is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
