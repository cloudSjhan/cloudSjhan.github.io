CatRec Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for CatRec

Last Updated: April 29, 2026

Overview

CatRec is a macOS screen recording application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This privacy policy explains what data CatRec accesses and how it is handled.

Data Collection

CatRec does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All recordings, screenshots, and settings remain entirely on your device.

Device Permissions

CatRec requests the following system permissions to function:

  • Screen Recording — Required to capture screen content. Granted through macOS System Settings.
  • Camera — Required for the camera overlay (picture-in-picture) feature. Only accessed when you enable the camera overlay. You can deny or revoke this permission at any time.
  • Microphone — Required for recording audio from your microphone. Only accessed when you enable microphone recording. You can deny or revoke this permission at any time.

These permissions are managed by macOS and can be changed at any time in System Settings > Privacy & Security.

Data Storage

  • All recordings and screenshots are saved locally to a folder you choose on your Mac.
  • Application preferences (language, camera shape, aspect ratio) are stored locally using macOS UserDefaults.
  • No data is sent to any server, cloud service, or third party.

Network Access

CatRec does not access the internet. The app functions entirely offline.

Third-Party Services

CatRec does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children’s Privacy

CatRec does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted here with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: cloudsjhan@gmail.com
